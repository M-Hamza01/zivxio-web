NUST One started from a simple frustration: NUST students juggle several separate portals just to check a timetable, a notice, or a grade. None of them talk to each other, and none of them are built for a phone screen first.

We wanted a single, focused app that brings the things students check daily — schedule, portals, notices — into one place, without trying to be everything at once.

## What we focused on first

For the first release, we deliberately kept scope narrow:

- **A clear daily view** — what's on today, without digging through menus.
- **Quick access to existing portals** — rather than rebuilding them, NUST One gets you there faster.
- **Notices in one feed** — so nothing important gets missed.

## What's next

NUST One is live and we're actively gathering feedback from early users. Future updates will focus on refining the timetable experience and expanding notification coverage.

If you're a NUST student and have feedback, [get in touch](/contact) — we read every message.
